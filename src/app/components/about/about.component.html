Dr. Heywood is a licensed psychologist who maintains a private practice in West Palm Beach,Florida specializing in the treatment of anxiety, mood, and interpersonal disorders with adults and children. He has practiced in Palm Beach County for over 20 years, working in schools, psychiatric facilities, and the private sector. In 1996, upon the completion of his Doctoral Clinical Internship at the Veterans Affairs Medical Center in Virginia, Dr. Heywood moved to Florida to serve his Residency training at a community psychiatric hospital in WPB. He continued his work the next 6 years providing neuropsychological evaluations, supervision, and psychotherapy intervention services.
